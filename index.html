The plan

From river bank to trail

Four phases carry the Riverwalk from first survey stakes to a finished, year-round path. Scroll to follow the river downstream.

Phase 01 · Underway

Survey & design

Walking the corridor, mapping the floodplain, and listening to neighbors. Locating the path where the river can rise without taking it back.

Phase 02 · Next

Grading & bridges

Shaping the trail bed, setting boardwalks over the wet reaches, and dropping in the footbridges that stitch the two banks together.

Phase 03

Surface & plantings

A firm all-season surface goes down; native willow, dogwood, and bunchgrass go in to hold the banks and bring back the shade.

Phase 04

Open the Riverwalk

Benches, signage, and trailheads at both ends. A continuous walk along the water, free and open to everyone, every season.
